We have every Facebook ad that is live at the moment saved in our database. We let you play with the filtering for those ads. A feature never seen before: Filtering on adset amount. You can find winning products which are just about to scale and crush them with better strategy.

Keep in mind that we have every ad that has ran or is running on Facebook, you might get the urge to filter on 20+ or 30+ adsets. In reality advertisers do not tend to duplicate one creative this many times to scale, they rather budget scale aswell. What we recommend is filtering on 6+ or 11+ adsets. Most people test with 5 adsets, if they are running more than 6 it means that they are scaling. If you want to find good products faster you might want to go for a higher number, 8-9-10-11+ but keep in mind to not go too high, we generally recommend not to go higher than 15+.

We suggest tracking the product's store that you want to test and searching for other competitors selling the same product in your country. If there are already numerous competitors running ads for the product in the country where you're advertising, we don't recommend running ads for it as well, as they benefit from Facebook's optimization. To analyze your competitors, check our Facebook ads section for keywords in the product name or ad text. Sometimes, trying translations can help uncover relevant keywords.

The available filters allow you to view only Dropshipping ads with visible store revenue. When you filter based on their scaling approach and the number of adsets they use for a creative, you can access performance-based analytics. Displaying likes or comment counts doesn't provide valuable insights since you can't determine their scaling strategy or whether the ad is still active.
